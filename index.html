Welcome to Kokkachal Wafy College

Kokkachal Wafy College is an Islamic Institution dedicated to providing Islamic Values and Academic Superiority. Our college is affiliated with the Coordination of Islamic Colleges (CIC). Located in Kasaragod, our college nurtures students in the fields of Social, Cultural, and Religious studies. Our faculty comprises experienced Lecturers who serve for the Upliftment of the College. We aim to provide Systematic Education for the students and equip them with the Skills and Knowledge required to excel in their future journeys.
